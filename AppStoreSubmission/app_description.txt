SoulFocus is a focused prayer and meditation companion designed for people of every spiritual tradition.

Whether you follow Hindu, Christian, Buddhist, Islamic, or universal spiritual practices — SoulFocus gives you a calm, distraction-free space to deepen your daily practice.

── WHAT YOU CAN DO ──

• Start a Meditation Session
  Choose your duration, breathing style, and ambient sound. A Tibetan singing bowl gently marks your session. The countdown ring keeps you grounded without checking the clock.

• Pray with Sacred Texts
  Swipe through beautifully formatted prayer cards with the original script, transliteration, and English meaning line by line. Traditions supported: Hindu, Christian, Buddhist, Islam, Universal.

• Chant with Rhythm
  Choose from a rich library of mantras and chants. A pulsing rhythm guide helps you stay with the tempo — slow, medium, or fast — so you can chant aloud with full focus.

• Track Your Streak & Progress
  Every completed session is recorded. Watch your streak grow and see your total time in practice at a glance from the home screen.

• Journal Your Sessions
  After each session, reflect on what you felt. Your session log keeps a record of your journey over time.

• 7-Day Free Trial
  Explore every feature free for 7 days. No commitment required.
