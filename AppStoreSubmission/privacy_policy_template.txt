Privacy Policy for SoulFocus
Last updated: March 2026

SoulFocus ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy.
This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

   SoulFocus stores the following data ONLY on your device (never on our servers):
   - Your selected spiritual tradition and preferences
   - Meditation and prayer session history
   - Your session streak and total practice time
   - Your journal entries
   - Your subscription status (managed by Apple)

   We do NOT collect:
   - Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
   - Location data
   - Health data
   - Device contacts, photos, or camera access
   - Any analytics or tracking data

2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

   - Apple In-App Purchases (StoreKit): Subscription purchases are handled
     entirely by Apple. We receive only a confirmation of purchase status.
     Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

3. DATA SHARING

   We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party.
   All data remains on your device.

4. DATA RETENTION

   All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework.
   Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

   SoulFocus is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children
   under 13. No personal information is collected from any user.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

   We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at
   this URL. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

7. CONTACT

   For privacy questions, contact: [your-email@domain.com]
