Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my free trial?
Your 7-day free trial begins automatically when you first launch SoulFocus. No credit card is required to start. You'll have full access to all premium features during the trial period.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SoulFocus → Cancel Subscription. You can cancel anytime and will retain access until the end of the billing period.
How do I restore my purchases on a new device?
Open SoulFocus → tap the upgrade/paywall screen → tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Why isn't the audio playing during meditation?
Check that your iPhone is not on Silent mode (the physical switch on the side). Also ensure SoulFocus has permission to play audio in the background via Settings → SoulFocus.
Which prayer traditions are supported?
SoulFocus supports Hindu, Buddhist, Christian, Islamic, and Sikh traditions, with tailored prayer modes and chanting for each. More traditions will be added in future updates.
Is my data private?
Yes. All your session history, journal entries, and streaks are stored locally on your device using CoreData. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.
