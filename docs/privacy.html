Our Commitment

SoulFocus does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information created within the app — including your session history, journal entries, streaks, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

✦ We do not have servers. We do not collect data. We do not track you.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's CoreData framework. This includes:

  • Meditation session history and duration
  • Prayer and chanting session records
  • Daily streak and practice statistics
  • Journal entries and reflections
  • Your selected spiritual tradition and app preferences

This data is yours. It never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

SoulFocus offers optional premium subscriptions (Monthly, Annual) and a Lifetime purchase. All transactions are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. SoulFocus does not handle, process, or store any payment or billing information.

No Third-Party Services

SoulFocus does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, Firebase, or any other third party embedded in the app.

Microphone & Permissions

SoulFocus does not request access to your microphone, camera, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device permissions. The only system capability used is audio playback for ambient meditation sounds.

Children's Privacy

SoulFocus does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.
