Meditation & Breathwork

Choose your duration (1 to 30 minutes), pick a breathing guide, set an intention, and begin. SoulFocus gets out of the way so you can go in.

  • Breathing Guides: Box breathing (4-4-4-4) for focus and calm, 4-7-8 for anxiety relief, Natural (5-7) for gentle ease, or No Guide for free awareness.
  • Ambient Sound: Singing bowl ambient audio with reverb — synthesised on-device, plays in the background even when your phone is locked.
  • Interval Bells: Gentle reminders at 2, 5, or 10 minute marks to bring a wandering mind back to the present.

Sacred Prayer Traditions

Speak from the heart. SoulFocus presents prayers in their original script alongside transliteration and translation — so you can connect deeply regardless of language.

  • Gayatri Mantra: Original Sanskrit (ॐ भूर्भुवः स्वः), transliteration, and full meaning — line by line.
  • 5 Traditions: Hindu, Christian, Buddhist, Islamic, and Universal prayers — each with its own visual identity.
  • Session Timer: A built-in countdown so you stay present without ever checking the time.
