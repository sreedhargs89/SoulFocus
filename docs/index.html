Meditation & Breathwork

Find your center with highly customizable meditation sessions designed to anchor your mind and relax your nervous system. Whether you have one minute or one hour, SoulFocus adapts to your needs.

  • Guided Breathing Cycles: Choose from Box Breathing (4-4-4-4), 4-7-8 Relaxation, or Awaken rhythms. An expanding and contracting visual orb intuitively guides the pace of every breath.
  • Ambient Soundscapes: Immersive, high-quality background audio tracks like 'Rainfall', 'Forest', 'Ocean', and 'Deep Space' quickly drown out external distractions.
  • Interval Bells: Enable soft, resonant singing bowl chimes at 1, 3, 5, or 10-minute marks to gently bring a wandering mind back to the present moment.
